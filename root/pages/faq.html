Frequently Asked Questions

Read this sections to learn more about our products, employees, and policies.

What is your service area?

We offer services in an approximate 100 mile radius of our Tijuana location.

How are your crews trained?

We train and certify all of our operators in-house so that they are prepared to safely deliver materials to your jobsite. We hold weekly training sessions on safety for all of our employees, and we even offer a safety incentive benefit program for our stockers and warehouse workers.

Do you offer before and after-hours and weekend delivery?

Yes, we can accommodate delivery requests.

What types of commercial deliveries do you provide?

We can deliver your materials using walk ups, walk downs, elevator roll-ins, buck hoist, and high-rise cranes.

Are you OSHA compliant?

Yes. We believe nothing is more important than worksite safety. We are committed to full compliance with OSHA and DOT laws and regulations, and we conduct regular jobsite visits to check for crew compliance.

Can I pick-up an order myself?

Yes. Customers can call orders in advance, or they can visit our showroom and speak directly with an employee about specific needs.
